About Me

Hi there! My name is Noah Boyd. I am a devoted student looking to expand my interest of computer engineering and coding with my diverse passions of music production and performance to create innovate solutions and enrich developing industries.

Education

I am currently pursuing an Academic Honors Diploma at Castle High School, maintaining a 4.0 GPA while taking an extensive list of AP and Dual Credit courses. As a Junior Marshall and a member of the National Honors Society, I am committed to academic excellence and leadership. After developing my skills through these diverse academic and extracurricular engagements, I am now ready to expand into higher-level education and college-level internships.

Experience

I am the section leader and student instructor for my school drumline, where I have honed my leadership and instructional abilities. My volunteer work with Boomsquad Inc., where our mission is to provide life learning skills with the integration of teaching music to underprivileged children, reflects my commitment to giving back to the community. My involvement in various coding bootcamps and as an intern in the Nextech Catapult program showcases my dedication to gaining real-world experience and honing my skills. I have also participated in varsity sports and Boy Scouts programs, demonstrating my versatility and dedication to teamwork. As a part-time musician, I play with multiple local bands of various genres and have experience recording and producing music for personal projects as well as local bands in the area.

Aspirations

My aspirations lie at the intersection of engineering and music. I aim to synthesize my technical skills in development coding with my musical talents to drive innovation in developing industries. I am particularly interested in machine learning and AI, and the implications of finding new applications for these fields within businesses. I am open to exploring various opportunities in internships, jobs, and further education to continue growing and achieving my goals.
